My Work

Machine Learning Researcher

In this role, I utilized a Scrum-style work environment to conduct research on machine learning methods for detecting and isolating weeds and strawberry plants. My responsibilities were as follows:

  • Researched and developed machine learning algorithms in Python code utilizing TensorFlow, capable of classifying and locating weeds and strawberry plants.
  • Collaborated with a team of students to deploy the machine learning models onto an aerial vehicle, which would communicate with a ground vehicle to provide real-time detection and removal.
  • Managed and analyzed large datasets of plant values to correlate ground truth with multispectral UAV values.

IT and Customer Service

In this position, I worked directly with the owner and workers of the business to manage spreadsheets and handle customer orders. My responsibilities were as follows:

  • Maintained all inventory and customer purchases, whether walk-in, phone call, or online.
  • Created online spreadsheets used to calculate costs, organize company inventory, and catalog orders.
  • Provided customer service to customers looking for specific items and ensured that sold items were removed from the inventory database and shipped.

Peer Tutor

This position allowed me to provide face-to-face assistance to other students who required help. I would have as many as four tutees in a given week. My responsibilities were as follows:

  • Visited classrooms as a speaker to provide assistance and guidance, as well as answer any questions students may have.
  • Provided learning assistance to students in any academic subject and homework.
  • Communicated one-on-one with students and collaborated with other tutors to help out the tutees.
